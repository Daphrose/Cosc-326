Example Cafe Recipes - World's Best Scrambled Eggs

I adapted this recipe from a book called Sydney Food by Bill Ginger.Ever since tasting this eggs on my 1st visit to Bill's restaurant in King's cross, Sydney, i have been after the recipe. I have since transformed it into what i really beleive are the best scrambled eggs i have ever tasted.

This recipe is what i call a "very special breakfast"; just look at the ingredients to see why. It has to be tasted to be beleived

Ingredients

The following ingredients make one serving

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of butter(10g)
  • 1/3 cup of cream(2 3/4 fl ounces)
  • A pinch of salt
  • Freshly milled black pepper
  • 3 fresh chives (chopped)

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs, cream, and salt in a bowl
  2. Melt the butter in a non-stick pan over a high heat(taking care not to burn in butter)
  3. Pour egg mixture into pan and wait until it starts setting around the edge of the pan (around 20 seconds)
  4. Using a wooden spatula, bring the mixture into the center as if it were an omelet ,and lt it cook for another 20 seconds
  5. Fold contents in again, leave for 20 seconds, and repeat until the eggs are only just done
  6. Grind a light sprinkling of freshly milled pepper over the eggs and blend in some chopped fresh chaives

You should only make a maximum of two servings pr frying pan
